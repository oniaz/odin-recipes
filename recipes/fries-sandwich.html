Fries Sandwich

Description

The classic Egyptian batatis (fries) mayonnaise sandwich you find at your regular falafel shop.

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Shami or fino bread
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Grated carrots
  • Baramili Cheese
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix an equal amount of ketchup and mayonnaise.
  2. Add to the mix the grated carrots and pickles.
  3. Open the bread and spread the Baramili cheese.
  4. Add the fries.
  5. Top with the ketchup and mayonnaise mix.
  6. Close the sandwich and enjoy!
Best enjoyed with a cola drink!
