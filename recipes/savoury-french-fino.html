Savoury French Fino

Description

Basically, it's savory french toast, but without toast because it's not always available for me, so instead I'll be using fino bread.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Fino bread
  • Shredded Turki cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Baramili Cheese
  • Milk
  • green olives
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter or ghee

Steps

  1. Cut the fino bread into slices so that you'll end up with circles.
  2. Crack the eggs in a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Heat the pan and add butter or ghee.
  4. Dip the bread slices in the egg mixture.
  5. Place the bread slices in the pan and cook until golden brown.
  6. Flip the bread slices and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. While still in the pan, spread the Baramili Cheese onto the slices, then sprinkle the mozzarella and turki cheeses, and green olives on top of the bread slices.
  8. Cover the pan and let the cheese melt.
  9. Once the cheese has melted, remove the bread slices from the pan and serve!

Tips

Best enjoyed with a cup of milk or chocolate milk!
