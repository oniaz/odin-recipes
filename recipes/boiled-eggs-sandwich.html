Boiled Eggs Sandwich

Description

My dad's special egg sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread of your choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Baramili Cheese or any spreadable cheese of your choice
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs and peel them.
  2. Chop the eggs and mix with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread the cheese on the bread.
  4. Add the egg mixture.
  5. Top with olives.
  6. Close the sandwich and enjoy!

Tips

Perfect with a cup of milk or chocolate milk!
